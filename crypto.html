Intro

I'm going to do the spring 2021 MasterMath Cryptology course from Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e). Part I is a self-contained, post-quantum cryptography course. Part II is cryptanalysis and all the lectures are open on YouTube, and the assignments are open to us. Tanja Lange the premiere researcher in these topics and Daniel Bernstein designed these courses as they are both department/research heads at TU/e now, djb is also on Team CASA some German think tank for cyber security in the age of large-scale adversaries.

I'll also work through the cryptopals challenges which are similar to part II of the MasterMath Cryptology course assignments, and require learning abstract algebra for the final set of challenges. Want to learn some abstract algebra and cryptanalysis for fun and profit?
